About

IEEE SB NCERC

IEEE SB NCERC was revamped on 7th January 2021 and was inaugurated by Susan Kathy Land, IEEE President 2021.Over these years IEEE SB NCERC could able to be a part of several events and also hosted our events. We provide members with the opportunity to meet and collaborate with other professionals in their field. Thus the members will get a chance to learn about the latest trends and developments in technology, as well as to build relationships with other professionals who can offer guidance and support. IEEE student branch NCERC pledged to inspire young minds through advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

Join IEEE 


IEEE membership offers access to technical innovation, cutting-edge information, networking opportunities, and exclusive member benefits.
